## Purpose
Correct valve operation is essential for safe plant start-up, shutdown,
isolation, and normal operations.

## Common Valve Types
- Gate valves
- Globe valves
- Ball valves
- Butterfly valves
- Control valves

## Manual Valve Operation
- Verify valve tag number and service
- Confirm direction of flow
- Operate valve slowly to avoid pressure shock
- Never force a stuck valve

## Control Valves (Field Awareness)
- Ensure instrument air availability
- Observe valve position feedback
- Watch for abnormal noise or vibration

## Line-Up Verification
- Double-check valve positions against P&ID
- Communicate with control room before and after changes
- Apply blinds or spades as per isolation procedure

## Safety Awareness
- Always follow PTW requirements
- Wear appropriate PPE
- Never bypass interlocks or safety systems
