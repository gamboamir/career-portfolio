## Objective
To ensure pumps operate safely, efficiently, and within design limits.

## Pre-Start Checks
- No visible leaks from seals or flanges
- Lubrication oil at correct level
- Cooling water available (if applicable)
- Suction and discharge valves in correct position
- Area clean and free of obstructions

## During Operation
- Monitor vibration and abnormal noise
- Check bearing temperature
- Confirm stable pressure and flow
- Observe seal leakage rate

## Common Abnormal Conditions
- Cavitation noise
- Excessive vibration
- Overheating bearings
- Seal failure

## Operator Action
- Inform control room immediately
- Reduce load or stop pump if required
- Follow PTW procedures before intervention
