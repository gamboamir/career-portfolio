## Purpose
To ensure safe, calm, and effective response during abnormal or emergency
conditions in industrial plant operations.

## General Principles
- Personal safety comes first
- Follow plant emergency procedures
- Maintain clear communication with control room
- Avoid panic and unsafe actions

## Common Abnormal Situations
- Equipment trips or failure
- Process leaks
- Power failure
- Fire or smoke
- High vibration or temperature alarms

## Immediate Field Operator Actions
- Identify the abnormal condition
- Inform control room immediately
- Evacuate area if required
- Isolate equipment if trained and authorized
- Prevent escalation where safe to do so

## Fire or Gas Release
- Raise alarm immediately
- Do not attempt unauthorized firefighting
- Move upwind where possible
- Follow emergency evacuation routes
- Assemble at designated muster point

## Power Failure (Blackout)
- Maintain situational awareness
- Avoid manual intervention without instruction
- Protect equipment from damage if required
- Assist in safe restart as per procedures

## Post-Incident Actions
- Record observations in shift log
- Participate in incident review if required
- Follow corrective actions and lessons learned
